Shenzhen Kangtai Biological Products (Chinese: 深圳康泰生物制品股份有限公司, SZSE: 300601), also known as BioKangtai (Chinese: 康泰生物), is a Chinese biopharmaceutical company that develops, manufactures and markets vaccines and other vaccine products.Kangtai develops the Minhai COVID-19 vaccine and has the exclusive right to manufacture the Oxford–AstraZeneca COVID-19 vaccine in mainland China.
