The Hellenic  Mathematical Society (HMS) (Greek: Ελληνική Μαθηματική Εταιρεία) is a learned society which promotes the study of mathematics in Greece. It was founded in 1918, and publishes the Bulletin of the Hellenic Mathematical Society among other research and educational publications. It is a member of the European Mathematical Society.
